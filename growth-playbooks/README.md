# How I Use AI Across the Growth Loop

This map draws on two roles that closely match the work I want to do: [Foresight Ventures' AI Growth role](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/M1tcoNIcypIYtlBFzynEQw) and [Antseer's User Growth Manager role](https://hire.rebase.network/jobs/1101.html). Together they provide a directional view of AI-enabled growth in early Web3 and AI products.

The map shows where I use AI in the growth process, what I still decide myself and the current evidence status for each stage.

**Sense → Frame → Produce → Match → Activate → Scale → Measure → Learn → Extend**

## How to read the labels

- **Shipped** — public code or output that a recruiter can inspect.
- **Applied at work** — used in real work; historical materials may be unavailable, reconstructed or sanitized.
- **Working prototype** — a runnable artifact without a verified market result.
- **Next experiment** — a defined test that has not yet produced market results.

Each claim carries its own status so shipped tools, work-applied methods and future experiments remain easy to distinguish.

## 1 · Sense

1. **Growth job:** Detect market, user and partner signals early enough to find a growth opportunity.
2. **What AI does:** Searches, summarizes and compares fragmented public information so more candidates and signals can be reviewed.
3. **What Melodie decides:** Which signal matters, whether it indicates partner fit, and whether it is strong enough to justify action.
4. **Existing proof:**
   - **Applied at work — partner opportunity research.** The OKX expansion case documents how public research supported prospect selection before human outreach and relationship building.
   - **Shipped — searchable KOL evidence.** A real sanitized query shows 89 matching posts across 27 unique sources in the private research corpus.
5. **Evidence status:** Applied at work for historical partner discovery; Shipped for the public retrieval output.
6. **Verification link / next experiment:** [Affiliate Partner Acquisition at OKX](../03-case-studies/okx-partner-expansion.md) · [Real KOL query output](../03-case-studies/kol-growth-intelligence-output.md)

## 2 · Frame

1. **Growth job:** Turn a signal into a testable audience, problem, value proposition and offer.
2. **What AI does:** Structures research into candidate briefs, competing explanations and possible offer angles.
3. **What Melodie decides:** The segment to pursue, the commercial logic, the evidence threshold and the condition for stopping.
4. **Existing proof:**
   - **Applied at work — partner-fit framing.** The reconstructed OKX example shows how market context became a partner hypothesis and first action.
   - **Shipped — partner decision.** The sanitized KOL brief records a B-priority decision, evidence for and against, a recommended offer, an outreach action and explicit upgrade, downgrade and stop conditions.
5. **Evidence status:** Applied at work for the historical operating method; Shipped for the public decision brief; outreach remains untested.
6. **Verification link / next experiment:** [Reconstructed partner decision example](../03-case-studies/okx-partner-expansion-reconstructed-example.md) · [KOL Partner Decision Brief](../03-case-studies/kol-partner-decision-brief.md)

## 3 · Produce

1. **Growth job:** Produce the smallest useful set of content, proposal and onboarding variants for a real growth test.
2. **What AI does:** Drafts controlled variants from one verified brief and reformats approved facts for different operating moments.
3. **What Melodie decides:** Which claim is supportable, which angle fits the audience, what must be removed, and what call to action is worth testing.
4. **Existing proof:**
   - **Applied at work — operating materials.** The two OKX cases show AI-assisted onboarding, competitor comparison and one-action campaign scripts.
   - **Next experiment — external content test.** A Coinbase AgentKit content experiment is fully specified but has not been published or measured.
5. **Evidence status:** Applied at work for historical operating materials; Next experiment for public content production and distribution.
6. **Verification link / next experiment:** [Partner example](../03-case-studies/okx-partner-expansion-reconstructed-example.md) · [Campaign daily-action example](../03-case-studies/campaign-operations-reconstructed-example.md) · [AI Content Experiment Playbook](./ai-content-experiment-playbook.md)

## 4 · Match

1. **Growth job:** Decide which KOLs, partners or user segments are worth pursuing and in what order.
2. **What AI does:** Retrieves comparable evidence and surfaces fit, mismatch and missing information.
3. **What Melodie decides:** A/B/C/D priority, the appropriate offer, the first outreach action and what would change the decision.
4. **Existing proof:**
   - **Shipped — callable KOL retrieval and a decision output.** Public code, a real sanitized query and a single-candidate sanitized brief show the path from evidence to priority.
   - **Applied at work — cross-sector affiliate acquisition.** The OKX operating case records real affiliate-partner acquisition results while keeping AI and human contribution separate.
5. **Evidence status:** Shipped for research and decision support; Applied at work for historical partner selection.
6. **Verification link / next experiment:** [KOL workflow and code](../01-kol-growth-intelligence/) · [KOL case](../03-case-studies/kol-growth-intelligence.md) · [OKX operating case](../03-case-studies/okx-partner-expansion.md)

## 5 · Activate

1. **Growth job:** Help a new partner or user reach a clearly defined first-value event.
2. **What AI does:** Converts complex rules into segment-specific onboarding material and one-action-at-a-time prompts.
3. **What Melodie decides:** The first-value event, the user segment, the intervention sequence and when human support is required.
4. **Existing proof:**
   - **Applied at work — first-trade activation.** During the campaign covering roughly 1,200 partners, AI-assisted scripts supported daily execution; the team ranked first among five teams and reached a 16–17% first-trade rate versus 5–6% for the other teams.
   - **Next experiment — instrumented product activation.** No public AI-enabled onboarding experiment currently connects individual exposure, onboarding behavior and first value.
5. **Evidence status:** Applied at work for campaign activation; Next experiment for product-level activation measurement.
6. **Verification link / next experiment:** [Campaign operating case](../03-case-studies/campaign-operations.md). The next experiment requires a product, first-value event, event data, user segments, observable cohort and an editable onboarding or notification touchpoint.

## 6 · Scale

1. **Growth job:** Keep partner operations clear and consistent when manual follow-up stops scaling.
2. **What AI does:** Helps turn changing rules and daily data into repeatable scripts, summaries and action queues.
3. **What Melodie decides:** Cadence, reward allocation, priority exceptions, suspected abuse handling and escalation.
4. **Existing proof:**
   - **Applied at work — campaign operations at roughly 1,200-partner coverage.** The operating case and reconstructed daily-action example show how the team moved from static rules to daily control.
5. **Evidence status:** Applied at work across a team campaign covering roughly 1,200 partners.
6. **Verification link / next experiment:** [Campaign operating case](../03-case-studies/campaign-operations.md) · [Reconstructed daily-action example](../03-case-studies/campaign-operations-reconstructed-example.md)

## 7 · Measure

1. **Growth job:** Define the funnel, compare variants and decide which action deserves more investment.
2. **What AI does:** Can assist with classification, comparison and anomaly review after events and attribution rules are defined.
3. **What Melodie decides:** The north-star and qualified events, the A/B rule, the minimum decision threshold and the attribution boundary.
4. **Existing proof:**
   - **Applied at work — growth measurement.** The campaign used daily tracking and ROI-based reward allocation.
   - **Next experiment — instrumented content comparison.** The content playbook defines UTM values, a qualified event and a minimum threshold before a winner can be declared.
5. **Evidence status:** Applied at work for operating measurement; Next experiment for AI-assisted attribution and A/B testing.
6. **Verification link / next experiment:** [Campaign measurement boundary](../03-case-studies/campaign-operations.md) · [Content measurement protocol](./ai-content-experiment-playbook.md#8-utm-structure-and-qualified-event)

## 8 · Learn

1. **Growth job:** Turn activation, retention and user feedback into the next product or growth decision.
2. **What AI does:** Can group feedback, summarize repeated friction and flag where cohorts diverge.
3. **What Melodie decides:** Whether the issue belongs to positioning, onboarding, product behavior or user quality—and whether to change, continue or stop.
4. **Existing proof:**
   - **Next experiment — AI-enabled activation and retention loop.** The required proof is a measured retention change following AI-assisted feedback analysis.
5. **Evidence status:** Next experiment.
6. **Verification link / next experiment:** Hold until there is a specific product, first-value event, readable event data, user segmentation, observable cohort and an onboarding or notification touchpoint that can be changed.

## 9 · Extend

1. **Growth job:** Make a useful product capability available inside the tools and ecosystems where developers or operators already work.
2. **What AI does:** Calls a structured capability from a conversational request and returns evidence in a reusable format.
3. **What Melodie decides:** Which job deserves packaging, the input and output contract, acceptance criteria, integration targets and adoption metric.
4. **Existing proof:**
   - **Shipped — callable KOL retrieval tool.** The public server exposes working search and listing functions with a smoke test and a real sanitized output.
   - **Next experiment — external adoption and repeat use.** There is no public evidence yet of external integrations, retained users or repeated third-party calls.
5. **Evidence status:** Shipped for callable tool packaging; Next experiment for ecosystem growth.
6. **Verification link / next experiment:** [MCP server and smoke test](../01-kol-growth-intelligence/mcp-server/) · The next proof is privacy-safe external use, integration or repeat-use evidence.
- It does not expose private KOL identities, posts, employer materials or internal operating files.
